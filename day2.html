How to make a sandwich
  1. Use 2 Multigrain bread.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on both sides of the bread
  3. Put on smoked organic turkey slices on bread
  4. Place organic ham slices ontop of the turkey slices
  5. Spread some honey mustard on top of ham
  6. Put tomato slices and romaine lettuce ontop
  7. Simple and easy, enjoy your delicious healthy sandwich!
